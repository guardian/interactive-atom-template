Editor's letterThis year the Design magazine goes quarterly, offering Observer readers four issues of architecture, innovation, art and product. To celebrate a new year and a new look to the magazine, this edition is dedicated to new horizons, exploring the best places to see great art and design around the world. From the eco art of Lanzarote to the new design quarter of Los Angeles, these destinations showcase creativity at its best. But one of the more amazing stories is in a much less picturesque location. Watchet in Somerset has the lowest social mobility in the UK, but soon it will be home to a company trying to develop plant-based concrete and bring new life to the town. 2020 is the start of a new decade. It’s time to think big. Alice Fisher
